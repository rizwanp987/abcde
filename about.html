About Us

At EduVerify, we are committed to making academic record verification and transcript applications simple, secure, and reliable. As a trusted online platform, we specialize in providing two key services for students, alumni, employers, and institutions:

  • Online Education Verification – Ensuring the authenticity of degrees and certificates issued by Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur (JNTUA) through a fast and transparent verification process.
  • Transcript Application Service – Helping students and graduates seamlessly apply for official transcripts from JNTUA without the hassle of lengthy paperwork or delays.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between universities, students, and organizations by offering a time-saving, paperless, and hassle-free solution. With EduVerify, every request is handled with the highest level of accuracy, security, and professionalism.

We aim to empower individuals and institutions by ensuring authenticity, transparency, and convenience in every step of the verification and transcript application process.
